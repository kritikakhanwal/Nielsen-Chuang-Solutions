\section{Q.2.17}
Any normal matrix follows the property $A^\dagger A= AA^\dagger$\\
$A^\dagger A= AA^\dagger$\\
$A^{-1} A^\dagger A= A^{-1}A A^\dagger$\\
$A^{-1} A^\dagger A= A^\dagger$..............(1)\\
for A to be hermetian it follows from eq (1) that: \\
$A^{-1} A^\dagger = \mathbb I  \implies  A^\dagger= A$\\
\newline
if A = $\sum\limits_{i}a_i\ket{i}\bra{i}$ , where $\ket{i}$ are the eigenvectors of the A and $a_i $ are the eigenvalues then,
$A^\dagger= A \implies \sum\limits_{i}a_i^*\ket{i}\bra{i} =$\sum\limits_{i}a_i\ket{i}\bra{i} $
$\implies a_i^* = a_i$, 
and this condition is only possible if a is real !
